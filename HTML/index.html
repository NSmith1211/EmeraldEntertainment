Featured Films

From Director Ryan Printy comes his debut short film which follows a college student's (Josh Banaszak) struggle in dealing with the news that the world is ending and his attempts to discover who he truly is in his final hours.
After discovering a large sum of cash tied to a body in the lake, three fishermen must decide how to proceed. As greed weaves distrust in the group, they battle with not only each other, but also a more sinister force. Avarice is the second short film by writer/director Ryan Printy.

Meet the Director/Writer

Ryan Printy, age 23, is a long-standing student of cinema, television, and the behind-the-scenes production of making film come alive on the silver screen. Beyond simply writing and directing his own movies Ryan spends countless hours further in post-production creating music, editing footage, and balancing audio levels. Mr. Printy's passion for filmmaking and commitment to autheticity and detail is inspired by one of his all-time favorite directors: Christopher Nolan.
After graduating from the Ohio State University in the class of 2020 with a bachelor's degree in City & Regional Planning, Ryan was accepted into the Film and TV Production Program at Loyola Marymount University in Los Angeles, California where he is currently a student. There he will continue to develop and master new techniques and abilities to produce more capitvating stories and cinematic works of art to bring to the silver screen.
